About Us

History

Originally formed in Cleveland, Ohio in 1946, our firm was known as Thomas & Associates. After a reorganization in 1952 the firm was renamed Thomas, Carpenter, Craig & Ziska. Allen Wapnick joined the firm in 1957. Brian Fialko and Lawrence Yunaska joined in 1983 and David Lerner became our firm's newest partner in 2005. The firm has been known as William Thomas & Associates since 1998.

Our team currently includes our four partners, a Firm Administrator, and six Accounting Team Members. Each team member has their own unique skills obtained through many hours of on-going educational training. This allows us to meet our clients' needs by having knowledgeable team members in every facet of the accounting and consulting areas.

Throughout our six decades in business, William Thomas & Associates has believed that our firm's foundation rests upon our relationships with our clients. We continue to view every client relationship as a partnership and we are committed to providing the highest level of professional and personal attention to each of them. We believe that our success is a result of our clients' success. Our clients receive advice that comes from years of advanced training and continuing education, technical expertise and financial acumen, state-of-the-art computer technology and world-wide business relationships.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help clients maintain financial viability in the present, while taking a proactive approach to achieve future goals. This requires open communication to reach an understanding of our clients' needs through research and sound analysis. William Thomas & Associates is dedicated to meeting these goals with high standards of excellence and professionalism. We have been a staple of the area's business community for over 60 years, and pride ourselves on the level of esteem we have earned.

Our dedication to hard work has earned the respect of the business and financial community in and around the area. We believe this to be a direct derivative of our talent and responsiveness to our client base. Whether you are a current or prospective client, rest assured that individuals and businesses who choose Baumgarten & Company receive competent and timely advice.

Our Core Values

  • Developing team members to help them achieve their full potential.
  • Provide exceptional service that focuses exclusively on tax, assurance and business consulting.

Our Promise

At William Thomas & Associates, we want to be clear with clients what they can expect when we agree to do business together. We take pride in working with clients to understand the elements of a successful business relationship. We make the following pledge to you:

  1. To understand your business—it is our first priority to understand the nature of your business, your operations, the industry you work in, and how to make your business successful. Our ability to serve you best is based on a thorough understanding of what you do.
  2. To plan our work with you—each client has a different way of working with their accountant. We will design our work plan around you, your staff, and your needs.
  3. To manage the work as if we were the client—putting ourselves in your shoes and thinking about the work from your point of view is critical. This approach allows us to schedule, communicate, and even charge our fees in a fair and cost-effective manner.
  4. To communicate according to your needs—effective and timely communication is one of the key factors in a successful relationship. We will understand your schedule, how often communication is needed, and work closely with you.
  5. To provide the quality you expect—as our mission statement says, providing quality service is what Pease & Associates is all about. You can count on us to have quality people on your engagement and to offer quality solutions to your business issues.
